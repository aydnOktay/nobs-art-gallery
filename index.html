We are Nobs

Nobs is a community-driven movement born on the Avalanche (AVAX) network. Together with the community, we built an unofficial points & reputation leaderboard that rewards real contributors who post and create positive content around AVAX.

Unlike bot-based systems, every point on Nobs is distributed by real human evaluators. The project evolved into a decentralized voting system where holders earn REP, vouch for other members, and grow the network organically, far beyond the original curators.

Nobs stands for transparency and fairness. Everyone who works hard, creates, and contributes gets rewarded for their effort. With strong community energy on the Avalanche network — a network you already know how big it is — Nobs has serious potential. Follow, support, and be part of the movement.
